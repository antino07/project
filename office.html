Installation Steps

  1. 1

    Purchase or Verify License

    Visit office.com, sign in with your Microsoft account, and purchase a subscription or enter your product key. Or visit office.com (Download Site), if you already have a product key or purchased a subscription.

    Note: Students may be eligible for free Office 365 through their school.

  2. 2

    Download the Installer

    Click "Download Now". The setup file will download automatically.

    File size: Approximately 70-100 MB depending on version.

  3. 3

    Run the Installer

    Locate the downloaded file (usually in Downloads folder) and double-click to run it. Accept any security prompts.

    If prompted by User Account Control, click "Yes" to allow the installer to make changes.

  4. 4

    Follow Installation Prompts

    The installer will guide you through the process. Installation typically takes 5-15 minutes depending on your system speed.

    Do not turn off your computer or interrupt the installation process.

  5. 5

    Activate Office

    Open any Office app (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), sign in with your Microsoft account, and Office will activate automatically.

System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or 11, or macOS 10.15 or later
  • 4 GB RAM minimum (8 GB recommended)
  • 10 GB available disk space
  • Internet connection for download and activation
  • Microsoft account for Office 365 subscription
